Traditional lemon pie

Description

Also known as Tarta María Luisa, is the asturian traditional way to prepare a delicious lemon pie.

You can find the original recipe here.

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of cookies
  • 150 g of butter
  • 70 g of grated coconut
  • 70 g of ground almonds
  • 6 eggs
  • 3 lemons strained juice
  • 2 small cans of condensed milk
  • 6 tablespoons of white sugar

Steps

  1. Crush the cookies and mix them with the butter, 2 tablespoons of condensed milk, the coconut and the ground almonds.
  2. When all the ingredients are well mixed, fill a removable mold, previously covered with baking paper. Put it in the oven at 180ºC, previously heated, for 15 minutes.
  3. Separate the yolks from the egg whites and set the whites aside. Beat the egg yolks with the condensed milk and lemon juice and set aside.
  4. Remove cake base from the oven, being careful not to burn yourself, and pour the egg yolk and lemon cream over the top. Put the cake back in the oven for another 15 minutes at 180ºC.
  5. Beat the egg whites until stiff and add the sugar gradually to make a meringue.
  6. Remove the cake from the oven and put the meringue on top of the cake. Put it back in the oven and turn off the temperature.
  7. Once the meringue gets a nice color, open the oven door and let it cool down. Once cool, put it in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  8. Enjoy!
